Gangtok

Gangtok, the jeweled crown of Sikkim, sits high in the Himalayas, where clouds kiss the earth. With monasteries humming ancient chants, its air is sacred, pure, and calm like a monk’s soul. MG Marg blooms like a painted poem, car-free and carefree, where every step feels like art. The mighty Kanchenjunga peeks through the sky, a silent guardian of peace and pride. Adventure dances in its rivers and trails — rafting, trekking, and paragliding for the brave-hearted. Tibetan flags flutter stories of faith and wind, adding colors to every corner.
